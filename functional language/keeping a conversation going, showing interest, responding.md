# Keeping a Conversation Going

- By the way, ...
- Could you tell me more about ...?
- What got you interested in ....?
- I've heard … - is that true?

# Showing Interest

- Wow, I never expected that!
- No way! That sounds amazing.
- Oh, really? That's interesting.
- Sounds great!

# Responding

- I can totally relate because ...
- I couldn't agree more.
- Absolutely / Certainly!
- That's exactly how I feel / think too.


# Dialogue: Yoga and New Experiences

**Kate:** Hey Alex! Long time no see! How have you been?

**Alex:** Hey Kate! I've been good, thanks! What about you? **I've heard** you started attending yoga sessions **- is that true?**

**Kate:** Yeah, it is. And you know what? It's been amazing! It's made a world of difference. I feel so centered and relaxed now.

**Alex:** **No way! That sounds amazing.** I've heard great things about yoga, too. But **what got you interested?**

**Kate:** Well, a friend invited me to a local yoga studio. I was hesitant at first, but I decided to give it a try. And I'm glad I did!

**Alex:** **I couldn't agree more.** Sometimes trying new things can lead to unexpected joys.

**Kate:** **Absolutely!** It's incredible how something as simple as trying out a yoga class can have such a positive impact. It's become a significant part of my routine now.

**Alex:** **I can totally relate to that because** I've had a similar experience.

**Kate:** Oh, really? **Could you tell me more about it?**
