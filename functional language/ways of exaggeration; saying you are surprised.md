# Ways of Exaggerating

- sick and tired of
- absolutely / completely / extremely
- outrageously / ridiculously
- more dead than alive
- tons of / mountains of / loads of
- tedious / mind-numbing

# Saying You're Surprised

- I can't believe my eyes / ears.
- I'm flabbergasted.
- I'm gobsmacked.
- It's beyond belief.
- I'm completely blown away.
- I'm staggered.

# Dialogue: Taking a Dog on a Plane

**Liam:** Hey, you won't believe the hassle I went through trying to take my dog on a plane!

**Oliver:** Really? What happened?

**Liam:** Oh, it was **completely** a nightmare! I had to gather **mountains of** documents, get **tons of** vaccinations, and fill out **loads of** forms. **I'm sick and tired of** this **tedious** process!

**Oliver:** **I can't believe my ears!** Honestly, **I'm staggered** by the amount of paperwork and requirements they demand.

**Liam:** Exactly! I mean, my poor dog looked **more dead than alive** after going through all those examinations.

**Oliver:** **It's beyond belief** that they subject pets to such hardships. They're like family to us, and it's heartbreaking to think of them going through all that stress.

**Liam:** Absolutely, Oliver. **I'm completely flabbergasted** by the insensitivity of it all.
