# Clothing and Fashion Vocabulary

## Adjectives to describe clothing

| Nr | Word            | Definition                                                                 |
|----|-----------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | artificial      | made by people, often as a copy of something natural                       |
| 2  | brand-new       | very new, never used before                                                |
| 3  | loose           | not fitting tightly; not fastened securely                                 |
| 4  | neat            | tidy and clean                                                             |
| 5  | pin-striped     | having thin lines that are close together                                  |
| 6  | polka-dotted    | having a pattern of small round spots                                      |
| 7  | old-fashioned   | not modern                                                                 |
| 8  | short-sleeved   | having short sleeves                                                       |
| 9  | scruffy         | untidy and looking a little dirty                                          |
| 10 | torn            | worn or damaged in parts                                                   |

## Verbs related to clothing

| Nr | Word             | Definition                                                                                          |
|----|------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 11 | dress up         | to dress (oneself or another) in your best clothes; to put on special clothes for special occasions |
| 12 | put on           | to dress yourself or someone else with an item or items of clothing                                 |
| 13 | wrap yourself up | to dress in warm clothes                                                                            |
| 14 | do up            | to fasten something                                                                                 |
| 15 | try on           | to put on a piece of clothing to see if it fits you                                                 |
| 16 | zip up           | to fasten an item of clothing using a zip                                                           |
| 17 | take off         | to remove, opposite of 'put on'                                                                     |
| 18 | wear out         | to use an item so that it becomes unfit for further use                                             |
