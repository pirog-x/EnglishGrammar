# Common rental phrases and words

| Nr | Word                    | Definition                                                                                                                                           |
|----|-------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | amenity                 | something that is provided for people's comfort or convenience, such as a swimming pool or restaurant                                                |
| 2  | autonomous heating      | a heating system that can be controlled without the need for human intervention                                                                      |
| 3  | estate agent            | a person or company that sells or rents houses, land, etc.                                                                                           |
| 4  | facility                | a physical structure or equipment that is used to provide a service, which is often necessary                                                        |
| 5  | first and last deposit  | the first and last payments made when renting a house or other property                                                                              |
| 6  | landlord                | a person who owns a house, building, or land and rents it to someone else                                                                            |
| 7  | lease agreement         | a legal document that sets out the rights and duties of a landlord and tenant                                                                        |
| 8  | maintenance and repairs | ongoing activities to maintain a flat or office in operating condition or when something gets damaged or broken                                      |
| 9  | move-in inspection      | (also known as a move-in condition report or a move-in walkthrough), is a process that takes place when you are about to move into a rental property |
| 10 | pet policy              | set of rules, regulations, and guidelines that a business, flat or restaurant, has in place regarding the acceptance and accommodation of animals    |
| 11 | sewage system           | the pipes and equipment used for carrying away wastewater from a bathroom                                                                            |
| 12 | tenant                  | a person who pays rent to live in a house, building, or land owned by someone else                                                                   |
| 13 | utility bills           | bills for electricity, gas, water, etc.                                                                                                              |
| 14 | white goods             | refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, etc.                                                                                                   |
