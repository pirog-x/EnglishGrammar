# Types of Clothing and Footwear

| Nr | Word             | Definition                                                                                                                                                                  |
|----|------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | blouse           | A shirt, typically loose and reaching from the neck to the waist.                                                                                                           |
| 2  | flip-flops       | A sandal consisting of a rubber sole fastened to the foot by a rubber thong fitting between the toes and around the sides of the foot.                                      |
| 3  | knitted sweater  | A sweater which is made using knitting equipment.                                                                                                                           |
| 4  | heavy-duty boots | Boots which are designed for tough duties to help keep the wearer safe, agile and comfortable.                                                                              |
| 5  | high-rise jeans  | Jeans having a fit at or above the belly button, and generally, the jeans rise measures 9–10 inches.                                                                        |
| 6  | low-rise jeans   | A type of pants that sit low on, or below, the hips.                                                                                                                        |
| 7  | overcoat         | A heavy garment worn over other clothes, for protection from cold or weather.                                                                                               |
| 8  | pencil skirt     | A slim skirt (clothing) with a straight and narrow cut, usually approximately knee-length.                                                                                  |
| 9  | pullover         | A sweater that must be put on by pulling it over the head; a sweater without buttons or a zipper in front                                                                   |
| 10 | pump shoes       | Exclusively refers to women's shoes with a kitten or higher heel.                                                                                                           |
| 11 | sneakers         | An athletic shoe with a soft, rubber sole.                                                                                                                                  |
| 12 | stiletto heels   | A woman's shoe with a thin, high tapering heel.                                                                                                                             |
| 13 | tights           | A close-fitting, sheer or non-sheer skin-tight garment worn principally by women and girls that covers the body completely from the waist down, usually including the feet. |
| 14 | V-neck cardigan  | A cardigan with a neck on a garment that comes down to a point on the throat or chest, resembling the shape of the letter "V".                                              |
| 15 | wellington boots | A waterproof boot that covers the foot and lower part of the leg, usually reaching almost up to the knee.                                                                   |
