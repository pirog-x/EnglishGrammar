# verb phrases to speak about habits and behaviour

| Nr | Word                   | Definition                                                                                         |
|----|------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | can’t stand            | to dislike; to hate someone or something                                                           |
| 2  | get on well            | to have a friendly relationship                                                                    |
| 3  | hang out with sb       | to spend a lot of time with someone                                                                |
| 4  | have a guilty pleasure | to have an activity that you enjoy but you would be embarrassed if other people found out about it |
| 5  | look up to sb          | to admire and respect someone                                                                      |
| 6  | keep in touch          | to continue to talk to or write to someone; maintain contact                                       |
| 7  | keep things private    | to maintain something in the secret state it is in currently                                       |
| 8  | keep to yourself       | you stay on your own most of the time and do not mix socially with other people                    |
| 9  | make up your mind      | to make a decision; decide                                                                         |
| 10 | socialise              | to participate in social activities; mix socially with others                                      |
