# nouns and adjectives to describe the weather

| Nr | Word                          | Definition                                                                 |
|----|-------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | blizzard                      | a severe snowstorm with strong winds and low visibility                    |
| 2  | downpour                      | a lot of rain falling in a short time                                      |
| 3  | drizzle                       | rain in very small, light drops                                            |
| 4  | hail                          | small, hard balls of ice that fall from the sky like rain                  |
| 5  | lightning                     | a flash of bright light in the sky                                         |
| 6  | puddle                        | a small pool of liquid, especially rainwater, on the ground                |
| 7  | shower                        | a short period of rain or snow                                             |
| 8  | sleet                         | a mixture of rain and snow or hail, typically falling in colder weather    |
| 9  | thunder                       | the sudden loud noise that comes from the sky especially during a storm    |
| 10 | boiling hot, scorching        | extremely hot                                                              |
| 11 | chilly                        | uncomfortably cool or cold                                                 |
| 12 | damp                          | slightly wet, especially in a way that is not pleasant or comfortable      |
| 13 | gloomy                        | dark in a way that is unpleasant and makes it difficult to see             |
| 14 | humid                         | (of air and weather) containing extremely small drops of water in the air  |
| 15 | overcast                      | with clouds in the sky and therefore not bright and sunny                  |
| 16 | stifling                      | hot and humid so that it is difficult to breathe                           |
