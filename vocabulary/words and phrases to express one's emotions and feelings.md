# Adjusting to a New Place / Culture

## Verb phrases

| Nr | Word                                | Definition                                                                                               |
|----|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | to adjust to smth                   | to get used to a new situation by changing the way you behave and/or think                               |
| 2  | to be charmed by                    | to feel a strong attraction or liking towards someone or something                                       |
| 3  | to become familiar with smth        | to learn about and become knowledgeable or experienced in something through practice, study              |
| 4  | to embrace the culture / rule       | to fully accept and appreciate the customs, traditions, and way of life of a particular group or society |
| 5  | to experience a feeling of euphoria | to feel an intense sense of happiness, excitement, and joy                                               |
| 6  | to find one's way                   | to get somewhere you are trying to reach                                                                 |
| 7  | to settle in somewhere              | to become comfortable and established in a new place, such as a home or community                        |
