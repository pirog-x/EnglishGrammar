# Phrasal Verbs and Useful Expressions

| Nr | Word             | Definition                                                                                                         |
|----|------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | benefit from     | to do something that is advantageous or good                                                                       |
| 2  | carry out        | to do or complete something, especially that you have said you would do                                            |
| 3  | end up           | to reach a particular place or achieve a situation after other activities                                          |
| 4  | make the most of | to use or enjoy something as much as possible                                                                      |
| 5  | pick up          | to learn something by watching or listening to other people                                                        |
| 6  | pop up           | to appear or happen, especially suddenly or unexpectedly                                                           |
| 7  | set off          | to start a journey                                                                                                 |
| 8  | take the plunge  | to decide to do something important or risky, especially after thinking about it for a long time                   |
| 9  | thrive on        | to enjoy or be successful in a particular situation, especially one that other people find difficult or unpleasant |
| 10 | turn up          | to arrive or appear somewhere, usually unexpectedly or in a way that was not planned                               |
